प्रिज्म के लिए विचलन का कोण

विचलन का कोण

(Angle of Deviation)

δ  =  α + β

=  (i - r) + (i’ – r’)

= (i + i’) – (r + r’)

 

(From geometry)

Angle of Prism

A = r + r’

So,

Angle of Deviation

विचलन का कोण

δ = i+ i’- A

 

Angle of Minimum Deviation(δmin)

Minimum deviation of ray

(δ = δmin) occurs only. When i=i’,

(Due to reversibility of path)

So, δmin = 2i–A

 

 

 

Relation between Refractive Index and the Angle of Minimum Deviation

अपवर्तनांक तथा निम्नतम विचलन के कोण मे संबंध

For minimum deviation,

न्यूनतम विचलन के लिए

i = i’ and r = r’

 

Minimum Deviation

δmin = 2i – A

Or,i = (A+ δmin) / 2

Also,since (A = r + r’)

r = A / 2

 

The refractive index ,

μ = sin i / sin r

For small angles sin θ = θ, Thus

Minimum deviation,

 δmin = (μ-1) A
