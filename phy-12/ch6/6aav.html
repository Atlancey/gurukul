MYOPIA

मायोपिया

Myopia or near signed person cannot see any object of far distance. His far point is not at infinity but it becomes closer.

 

Cause of myopia

मायोपिया के कारण

 

  • The eye ball elongated along its axis

 

  • The focal length of the eye lens becomes short

 

  • नेत्र गोलक के आकार में वृद्दि

 

  • नेत्र लेन्स का फोकस दूरी काफी  कम होना

Correction of myopia

मायोपिया का निवारण

 

इस दोष का निवारण ऐसे अवतल लेन्स का प्रयोग करके किया जा सकता है जिसका फोकस दूरी पिड़ित आँख के दूर बिन्दु F के बराबर है ।

 

Q. A particular nearsighted person is unable to see objects clearly when they are beyond 2.5 m away (the far point of this particular eye). What should the focal length be in a lens prescribed to correct this problem?

Ans: To remove above problem, we require a lens such that if any object is located at infinity then the lens should make its virtual image at the far point of the defected eyed person. Because if anything placed beyond far point, the defected eye will not be able to see it clearly.

Hence in this case object distance p = ∞ and q = -2.5m.

Q. What is the power of this lens?

Similarly consider a person with far point of 5 meters,

Would need contacts with focal length f = –5 meters.

 

Doctor’s prescription reads:

P = 1 / (-5m) = –0.20 Diopters
