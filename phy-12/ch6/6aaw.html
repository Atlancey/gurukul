Hypermetropia

हाईपर्मेट्रोपिया

In hypermetropia person do not able to see an object which is close to eye. The near point is not 25cm it become far

 

Cause of hypermetropia

हाईपर्मेट्रोपिया का कारण

  • The eye ball becomes too small along its axis.
  • The focal length of the eye lens become its large.
  • नेत्र लेन्स का आकार छोटा हो जाना.
  • नेत्र लेन्स का नाभ्यान्तर बढ़ जाना.

Correction of hypermetropia

हाईपर्मेट्रोपिया का निवारण

 

A hypermetropia eye is corrected by using convex of suitable focal length.  इस दोष का निवारण के लिए उपयुक्त नाभ्यान्तर उत्तल लेन्स का प्रयोग किया जाता है ।

 

Q. Find focal length if near point of any defected eye is 50 cm.

Ans: To remove above problem, we require a lens such that if any object is located at 25cm then the lens should make its virtual image at the near point of the defected eyed person. Because if any object placed before near point, the defected eye will not be able to see it clearly.

Hence in this case object distance u = 25cm and v = -50 cm
